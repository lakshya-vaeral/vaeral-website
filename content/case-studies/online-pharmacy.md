problem: |
  When health-conscious Indian consumers searched for this e-pharmacy online, what they found wasn't reassuring. Reddit threads questioned its legitimacy. Quora answers were riddled with skepticism and often allegations. The worst part is that "scam" and "fraud" keywords were actively ranking alongside the brand name on Google.

  For a pharmacy asking people to trust it with their health, this wasn't just a reputation problem. It was a conversion problem, a retention problem, and an existential one.

  **62% of brand sentiment across community platforms was neutral or negative** before Vaeral stepped in.
whatWeDid: |
  We audited every platform where health communities were actively forming opinions on Reddit, Quora, Google and built a systematic counter-narrative strategy rooted in authenticity, not advertising.

  Real user experiences were surfaced and amplified. Expert-level Quora answers positioned the platform as doctor-verified and medically credible. Reddit presence was built steadily across health and wellness communities where purchase decisions are quietly made every day.

  We positioned the app as not only a trustworthy option but also as one of the best in the market through subtle integrations and authentic posts solving real user problems.
results: |
  - Positive sentiment climbed from 38% to **84%** across 400+ community mentions.

  - **3.2× increase** in organic visibility across Reddit and Quora.

  - Reddit scaled to 68 live posts, generating 699,154+ views, 1498+ upvotes, 1187+ comments.

  - Captured Google Featured Snippets for high-intent online pharmacy search queries.

  - Brand successfully repositioned as a trusted, doctor-verified platform in consumer conversations.

  - Multiple Reddit assets indexed and ranked on Google AI Overview, ChatGPT and other AI platforms for medicine review, legitimacy, delivery, substitutes, and discount searches

  From "Is this even legit?" to the brand people recommend in health threads, in under six months.
